Permissions We Use and Why

MyFellowPet requests only the permissions necessary to deliver essential features of the app. We do not use any permission in the background or without your explicit action. Below is a clear explanation of each permission and why it is required:

1. Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION & ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)

Purpose: To help users discover the nearest available pet service providers based on their current location. This improves search accuracy and user convenience.

We do not track continuous location or store exact movement data.

2. Camera (CAMERA)

Purpose: Required for upcoming features that allow users to take photos of their pets to upload as profile photos and for sharing images with boarding service providers.

The camera is only activated when the user manually opens the camera inside the app.

3. Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO & MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS)

Purpose: Used to enable voice messages during the final step of the boarding booking process when chatting with the service provider. Audio is recorded only when the user taps and holds the microphone button—never in the background.

4. Storage / Media (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_DOCUMENTS)

Purpose: To allow users to upload photos, videos, or documents from their device (e.g., pet images, pet vaccination certificates, or house photos for service providers).

We cannot access any files unless the user manually selects them.

5. Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)

Purpose: To send booking alerts, chat notifications, payment confirmations, and general updates related to your app activity. Users may disable notifications at any time.

6. Bluetooth (BLUETOOTH)

Purpose: Reserved for optional future features involving Bluetooth-enabled pet accessories. It is not used unless the user manually connects a device.

7. Internet (INTERNET)

Purpose: Required for all core app functions such as booking, chatting, uploading files, and viewing service provider details.

5. Cookie Policy

This Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies when You access and use Our Platform. It is part of Our Privacy Notice and Terms of Use. Any special terms here have the same meanings as in the Privacy Notice unless stated otherwise.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are tiny data files saved on Your device or browser by websites and apps. They help Us recognize You, remember Your choices, and improve Your overall experience. Cookies can be:

  • First-party cookies: Set directly by Our Platform during Your visits.
  • Third-party cookies: Set by other companies, like advertisers or analytics providers, when You interact with the Platform.

They can also be categorized by how long they last:

  • Session cookies exist only while You’re browsing and are deleted once You close Your browser.
  • Persistent cookies stay on Your device for a longer period or until You delete them, so We can identify You on future visits.

Why We Use Cookies?

We use cookies to:

  • Confirm Your identity and secure Your access
  • Remember Your preferences and settings
  • Analyze usage patterns to make the Platform better
  • Assess how well Our ads and marketing efforts perform
  • Personalize content and enhance Your user experience
  • Enable essential Platform features

Our cookies include various types such as functional, performance, advertising, and social media cookies. We may add or change cookie types to keep improving Our services.

Controlling Cookies

You have the option to manage cookies through Your browser settings, where You can delete or block them. Keep in mind, disabling some cookies may limit the functionality or convenience of the Platform. For more on cookie management, check Your browser’s support or reliable online guides.

Do We Use Third-Party Cookies?

Yes. We work with trusted third-party service providers who may place cookies on Your device when You use the Platform to help deliver their services, such as analytics, advertising, or social media features. Additionally, You might receive cookies directly from these third parties during Your visit. For more details about these cookies and their purposes, please refer to the respective third party’s privacy or cookie policy.
