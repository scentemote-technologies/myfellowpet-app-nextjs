How It Works

Booking with MyFellowPet is quick, simple, and built around your convenience. Just pick the service you need, select the dates, and instantly discover trusted service providers near you. Each provider has a verified profile with all the essential details, so you can choose the right fit for your pet with total confidence.

Once you find someone that fits your needs, go ahead and place a booking request. You’ll be having a direct conversation with them to confirm availability, share expectations, and mention any special instructions your pet may require. Once everything’s aligned, the service provider will accept the request, and your booking will be good to go - secure, smooth, and confirmed.

But what if things don’t go as planned? What if the provider doesn’t accept the request or becomes unavailable?

Don’t worry! We’ll step in to help you find someone else quickly. Your pet will never be left alone.

Because for us, your pet’s safety, comfort, and happiness will always come first.
