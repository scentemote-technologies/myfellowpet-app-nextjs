Get started as a MyFellowPet Service Partner

Becoming a Service Partner with MyFellowPet is simple and fully guided. Start by signing in through our official partner portal.

How to begin

  • Visit partner.myfellowpet.com
  • Sign in securely using your Google account
  • Your partner account is created instantly

Choose or create your service profile

After signing in, you’ll be guided based on whether you already have an existing service profile.

What happens next

  • If you already have a profile, select it from the profile list
  • If you’re new, create a new service profile
  • Choose the service that best fits you, such as Home Boarding

Complete your profile in four steps

Your profile setup is broken into clear stages to ensure accuracy and easy completion.

Profile setup stages

  1. 1 Basic Details
  2. 2 Service Information
  3. 3 Dashboard Setup
  4. 4 Review & Confirmation

Review, submit, and go live

Once all details are completed, review your information and submit your profile to go live.

Final steps

  • Review all entered details on the final page
  • Click Complete Profile
  • You’ll be redirected to your dashboard automatically
